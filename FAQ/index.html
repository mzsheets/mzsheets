Is MZsheets a shop?

No. MZsheets is not a shop, It's a catalog of product links. Think of it like a newspaper, the newspaper does not sell the products it shows, it just directs users to their real listings.

What is a "cnfans sheet"?

Cnfans sheets are collections of product links that direct you to items on cnfans. Usually these are poorly made google docs, but MZsheets organizes these links for easy browsing.

How do I use MZsheets?

Register on cnfans.com, browse items on MZsheets, and add them to your basket on cnfans.com to complete your purchase.

What is cnfans?

Cnfans is a popular chinese shipping agent, allowing anybody across the globe to buy products in china, and have them shipped directly to your door.
